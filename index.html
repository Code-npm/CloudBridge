Our Aim

Our project aims to create an IoT-powered food freshness detector that monitors the quality of stored food in real time. By tracking temperature, humidity, and gas levels, the system can sense early signs of spoilage. It sends instant alerts to users through a connected website. This smart solution helps in reducing food waste and maintaining freshness efficiently.
